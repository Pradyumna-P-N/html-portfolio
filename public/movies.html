The Best Movies According To Me

My top 3 movies of all time.

Demon Slayer : Infinity Castle Arc

Best animation according to me. it was so immersive and incredible to watch. the animation was smooth and neat. The individual battles were breathtaking. Zenitsu and Kaigaku, Giyu, Tanjiro and Akaza and of course, Shinobhu and douma; presented very well.

Star Wars : Revenge of the Sith

This movie made me instantly love the prequels and the main movies. I had watched this first, even before I watched the episodes 1 and 2. The transformation of Anakin and the battle between Obi-wan and Anakin had me thrilled!

Interstellar

Great depiction of the unknown. Be it the water planet, or even the black-hole. The conflict between the main character's responsibility [as a father] and his passion is shown gorgeously! definitely top 3 movie for me.
